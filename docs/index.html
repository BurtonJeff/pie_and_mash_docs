What You Can Do

Find Shops

Browse a map or list of traditional pie & mash shops across the UK, sorted by distance.

Check In

Visit a shop, check in within 200 metres, and snap a photo to earn points and badges.

Earn Badges

Hit milestones like your first visit, 5 different shops, or 10 total check-ins to unlock badges.

Climb the Leaderboard

Compete on all-time and weekly leaderboards ranked by points. First visits earn 35 points.

Join Groups

Create or join groups with invite codes. Chat, organise Meatups, and set group challenges.

Take on Challenges

Complete global challenges from the team or group challenges set by your mates.

Our Story

Two brothers meet up for pie & mash whenever one of them flies into London. One day they walk into Manze's on Tower Bridge Road and sit down with a bloke called Norman — a man who loves pie and mash so much he's been to every shop in the UK and written a book about it. After a lovely lunch and great chat, the idea for the app was born.
